About Me

I am Nghe-Nhan

My journey into the world of science and technology began early, sparked by a profound passion ignited during my high school years as the president of the Science and Technology Club. This early enthusiasm flourished further when I entered university and joined the Intelligent System Laboratory (ISLAB) at HCMUTE, where I discovered a captivating drive for Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning research. I graduated from HCMUTE with the highest thesis score in my major (9.1/10), a testament to my dedication and foundational expertise.

Building on this strong academic background, I further immersed myself in cutting-edge research at the Imaging and Intelligent Systems Laboratory (IISLAB) at Sejong University. Here, as an Assistant Researcher and Teaching Assistant to Professor Seong G. Kong, I've deepened my understanding and practical skills. My anticipated graduation for my Master's degree is in August 2025.

My primary research interests include: Deep Learning-based Video Surveillance, Multimodal Image Fusion & Registration, Object Detection, AI Deployment on Edge Device

I am always eager to contribute my analytical skills, research acumen, and a proactive approach to innovative projects in the field of AI and Computer Vision.
